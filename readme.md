This Application is built as a form of food shuffler, It's for the people that love cooking food but do not know what to cook on which day. The application help users shuffle the meals that they have in mind and provide them with a given day. In other cases this application can be also used in other cases lets say in a family there is a rule each family cooking on turns. The came of the individual can be listed on the input block after clicking on your day of choice and the Mealshuffle app will provide each persons name and the days asigned for them to cook.
